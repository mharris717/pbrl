Team Roster
 
  Tip  
  Prior to completing any player transactions, your league's rosters must first be filled. This can be done in the following ways:

On-line Draft
Teams take turns selecting players, either in one day while everyone in your league is on-line, or over a period of several days using an extended draft.

Automated Draft
Each team will rank players, then players will be assigned to rosters on draft day using an automated draft process.

Off-Line Draft
Enter your off-line draft results, or simply decide which players belong on which team.

Auction Draft
Teams put players up on the auction block with an opening bid. Once a player has been won, the winning bid is subtracted from the winning team's salary cap. You may view a demonstration of the auction room.
